Contact

Before contacting me please check that your question is not answered somewhere on the website.

When contacting about bugs or problems getting the utility to compile please provide as much information as possible, including:
  • the operating system being used
  • the version of Playrec, PortAudio and any SDKs being used;
  • the version of Matlab;
  • the compiler being used;
  • all options used when compiling the utility (which checkboxes are selected and what is in the text boxes);
  • all compilation output in the Matlab Command Window, ideally compiling in verbose mode.
Please send all support request emails to support@playrec.co.uk and all other emails to info@playrec.co.uk.
